Secret Panissa Recipe Unlocked!

  • 300g chickpea flour
  • 1L water
  • 10g salt
  • Oil for frying
  1. Whisk sifted chickpea flour, salt, pepper, and water together until smooth and lump-free.
  2. Heat the mixture in a pot over very low heat for about 80 minutes, stirring constantly until it becomes thick and pulls away from the sides.
  3. Press the thick dough evenly into an oiled loaf pan, cover, and refrigerate for 2 hours until firm.
  4. Unmold and cut into 1-inch (2-3 cm) cubes. Deep-fry in 170°C (340°F) oil for 5 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

About Us

We are the official Capture the Flag hacking team of the University of Genoa. Composed of Bachelor's and Master's students united by a passion for cybersecurity, we actively improve our skills in national and international competitions.

We also organize a bootcamp called Pwnissa Academy during the first semester of university, where we teach hacking basics to any student who wants to participate.

Our name comes from pwn + panissa, a traditional Ligurian street food made from fried chickpeas.
