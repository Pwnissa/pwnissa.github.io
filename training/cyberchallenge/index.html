The Program

CyberChallenge.IT is Italy's main training program for finding and training new cybersecurity experts. It targets students and young adults between 16 and 24 years old.

Who Can Join?

You don't need to be a programming expert to join. The main requirements are curiosity and a willingness to learn. We look for people who enjoy solving logic problems and figuring out how things work.

National Recognition

The Italian Ministry of Education has recognized CyberChallenge.IT as a project for the "valorization of excellence" since 2020. The ministry uses the program to find and reward top-performing high school students.

The National Cybersecurity Agency (ACN) also includes the program as part of its 2022-2026 National Cybersecurity Strategy.
