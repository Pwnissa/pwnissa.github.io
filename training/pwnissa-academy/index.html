Our Mission

The goal of Pwnissa Academy is to introduce students at the University of Genoa to the fundamentals of cybersecurity, fostering a community of enthusiasts who learn, hack, and grow together. We achieve this through a blend of theoretical lectures and interactive laboratories tailored to build the skills necessary for Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions.

The Role of CTFs

In recent years, Capture The Flag competitions have become a cornerstone of the international cybersecurity landscape. They are a primary scouting and training tool for industry leaders like Google, Meta, Microsoft, and Cloudflare. Universities worldwide have built strong reputations through the successes of their student CTF teams, leading to global recognition and corporate partnerships. CTFs are more than just games; they are a direct bridge between academia and the professional cybersecurity industry.

Pwnissa Academy: 2025 Edition

Our 2025 edition, concluding in mid-December, was a major success. We averaged 60 attendees per lecture and registered over 70 active users on our custom training platform. The cohort included students from both Bachelor’s and Master’s programs in Computer Science and Computer Engineering.
